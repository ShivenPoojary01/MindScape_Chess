About MindScape Chess

Building grandmasters from the ground up.

Founded in Mumbai, MindScape Chess is more than just a chess academy. We are a community of passionate players, analysts, and competitors. Our philosophy is simple: we don't just teach you how the pieces move; we teach you how to think, analyze, and dominate the board.

Whether you are touching a pawn for the first time or grinding past a 1500 rating plateau, our structured curriculum and engine-backed analysis ensure you are always making the best move.
